    After completing his 5 years stint with Price Waterhouse Coopers in year 2010 he became Partner with PDK & Co, Mumbai. As a Partner he completed 6 years stint with PDK & Co, Mumbai. Originally a native from Indore he moved his base from Mumbai to Central India, Indore. He established his own CA Firm JG & Company (Membership No. 131214, FRN: 021766C). He is an Ind AS Consultant and has successfully converged Ind AS financials for more than 12 companies both in Indore and Mumbai.
